About Me

Hi, I am Refilwe M. Mathebula, a dedicated Public Health Professional with over 11 years of progressive experience in HIV Testing Services (HTS) and community-based program coordination across NGOs and clinical settings. Proven expertise in client-centred counselling, HIV prevention education, and peer-led mentorship programs to support vulnerable populations while applying the South African National T-95 Strategy and LIVES approach.

Adept at training and supervising HTS teams, implementing testing protocols, and linking clients to care while maintaining confidentiality and ethical standards. Passionate about stigma reduction, community mobilization, and advocating for key populations to improve health outcomes. Strengths include data-driven program improvement, multi-stakeholder collaboration, and culturally sensitive service delivery.
